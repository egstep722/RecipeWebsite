  • Panera Bread

    Looking for soup and a salad? Panera Bread offers a lot of customizeable soups and sandwiches to fit your diet!


  • Taco Bell
    Taco Bell offers quite a few heavily cheesed options, specific locations even have a vegetarian menu mode!
  • Chipotle
    This fast and exciting restaurant offers mexican style bowls and burritos. It has filling meals for vegetarians that include guacamole and even plant based meat!
  • Sabor Latin Street Grill
    Sabor offers so many vegetarian options from nachos to empanadas. Coming here you will definitely find foods that meet your needs and are super tasty!
